Self-Labeled Preference Transfer (SPT) is a two-stage VLA that co-trains a preference-conditioned action head and a preference-recognition head, then self-labels unlabeled target demonstrations to transfer preference-following to new tasks &mdash; reaching 96.4% recognition accuracy and +25.4% preference-following over a naive VLA baseline in both simulation and real-world tasks.
